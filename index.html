About Me

As a Mechanical Engineering student at NIT Trichy, I am passionate about exploring the intersection of Material Science, Energy, and IT to create innovative, sustainable technologies. My academic journey has equipped me with skills in computational modeling, energy systems, and programming, which I have applied to projects such as AI-driven material property prediction and smart grid optimization.

I thrive in interdisciplinary environments where I can merge my technical expertise with problem-solving creativity. My interests lie in developing advanced materials for renewable energy, leveraging AI for materials research, and contributing to smarter, more efficient energy systems.

Currently, I am actively seeking opportunities in research, internships, or collaborations that align with my goal of addressing global challenges in energy and sustainability. My curiosity, drive for innovation, and commitment to lifelong learning define my approach to work and learning.
